We make it simple. Connect your platforms and read all your chats, activity feeds,

## Other Features

- **Cross-Platform Commands:** Create a `!socials` command once, and it works on Twitch, YouTube, and Kick.
- **Spotify Integration:** Show your viewers what you're listening to with chat commands.
- **Custom Bot Name:** Use your own account as the bot name for a professional look.
