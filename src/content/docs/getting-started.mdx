Welcome to Synchra, the all-in-one multistreaming tool designed to simplify your life as a content creator. Whether you stream on Twitch, YouTube, Kick or TikTok, Synchra unifies it into one single dashboard.

## What is Synchra?

Synchra is a browser-based suite of tools that helps you manage multiple streams without installing any software.
It connects directly to your platforms to provide:

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Unified Chat" icon="comment">
    Read and reply to messages from all platforms in one window.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Activity Feeds" icon="list-format">
    Track follows, subs, and donations in real-time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automation" icon="setting">
    Set up commands, timers, and alerts that work everywhere.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to Start

<Steps>

1. Go to the [Synchra Dashboard](https://dash.synchra.net).

2. Sign in with your primary streaming account (e.g., Twitch or YouTube).

3. Connect all your streaming platforms by clicking on the connect button for each platform.

4. That's it! You're now up and running and can read all your chats in one place.

</Steps>
